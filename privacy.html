Privacy Policy

Effective March 14, 2026

Soulvy LLC ("we," "us," or "our") builds iOS applications designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains how our apps handle your data.

Apps Covered

This privacy policy applies to all Soulvy apps:

Kairos Crisper FlexTime ListFlow DecisionFlow HourFlow HydroFast

Data Collection

Our apps are designed with a privacy-first approach. We collect no personal data. All data you create within our apps — including settings, preferences, habits, and records — is stored locally on your device.

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

iCloud Sync

Some of our apps offer optional sync via Apple's CloudKit (iCloud). When enabled, your data is synced securely through your personal iCloud account. This data is managed entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

On-Device Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. If you delete an app, all locally stored data associated with that app is also deleted.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we do not collect data from any users, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
